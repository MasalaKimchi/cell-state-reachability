Cell-State Reachability — interactive explorers

A convex-cone oracle that decides, from measured CRISPRi Perturb-seq effects, whether a target cell state is reachable by a non-negative mix of knockdowns — and, when it is not, names the genes that must be activated instead. These explorers open the method, its causal-inference foundations, and its impact on drug-development decisions.

Seven single-file explorers. Each is self-contained — no server, no libraries — and every number is computed by reachability.py on the real effect matrix and cross-checked against the repo's result tables.
